What it covers

Eight analytical modules built from CBK published data, updated as new data becomes available.

Loans

Outstanding balances, new lending flows, customer and sector breakdowns, maturity structure.

Deposits

EUR deposit stocks and flows, segment splits, time deposit origination, maturity bucketing.

Interest Rates

Lending and deposit rates, spread analysis, household vs corporate pricing, WACF proxy.

Cards & Payments

Card issuance, terminal infrastructure, transaction volumes, cashless adoption trends.

Remittances

Inflow channels, country concentration, MTO share, quarterly and monthly trends.

Profitability

Income composition, cost-to-income ratio, NIM proxy, fee dependency, net profit margin.

Credit Risk

Loan loss provisions, cost of risk, provisioning intensity from cumulative YTD data.

Asset Structure

Loan-to-asset ratio, securities allocation, domestic credit vs foreign asset trends.

Methodology

All data is sourced from the Central Bank of Kosovo public statistical tables. KPIs are computed transparently — every chart and metric documents its formula, data source, and known limitations. No proprietary models or black boxes. The dashboard includes a full methodology page with KPI definitions, caveats, and data coverage.
